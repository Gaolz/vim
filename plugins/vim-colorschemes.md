vim-colorschemes
================

> 项目主页：[https://github.com/flazz/vim-colorschemes](https://github.com/flazz/vim-colorschemes)

> 订制 vim 颜色主题

### Using
---------

> .vimrc

- colorscheme [nameofcolorscheme]
- colorscheme wombat
- colorscheme molokai

> inside vim, you use:

- :colorscheme molokai
